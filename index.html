The mission of the WFC is to educate people of all ages, abilities and backgrounds so they can become active stewards of the marine environment, and to promote community sailing and other environmentally friendly ways to enjoy the waters of Oyster Bay.

Best For:

Quick & easy access to water-based activities including sailing, kayaking, and stand-up paddle boarding.

The WFC is largest community sailing center in New York, and has training programs for adult & junior sailors throughout the spring, summer, and fall.

Oakcliff Sailing Center is a unique organization dedicated to raising the level of sailors and sailing in the United States through match racing, coaching, and the intensive Acorn & Sapling programs.

Best For:

Experience sailors looking to improve their skills, and competitors looking to race against international competition.

Recently, Oakcliff has acquired a fleet of high-performance dinghy's and has become a US Sailing Olympic Training Center.

Programs

  • Sapling & Acorn Training Program
  • Match Racing
  • One-Design & IRC Racing
  • Corporate Events

Seawanhaka Corinthian Yacht Club is a full-service, year-round yacht club dedicated to the spirit of Corinthian sailing. Located on Centre Island, near enough to Manhattan to be convenient, but yet a world away. Home to an active keelboat team racing and junior sailing programs.

Best For:

Historic club with a large sailing and social calendar
